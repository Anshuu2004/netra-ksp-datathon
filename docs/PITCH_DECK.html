The problem

Rich data, locked away. Policing stuck in reaction.

🗄️ Data silos & Excel

FIRs, accused, victims and networks sit in fragmented sheets. Querying needs SQL skills and English fluency.

🔌 No advanced analytics

Behavioural patterns, organized-crime networks and hidden links go undiscovered.

🌐 Information gaps

SCRB gets fragmented inputs — no comprehensive, state-wide intelligence picture.

⏱️ Reactive, not proactive

Without trend exploration, there are no tools for prevention or evidence-based deployment.

The solution

One question → one explainable, visual answer.

Every reply is a living visual and an evidence trail back to the exact FIRs used.

Conversational + voice

Type or speak, English or Kannada. Context-aware follow-ups. Spoken answers.

Visual answers

Hotspot maps, criminal-network graphs, risk dossiers, money trails, trend charts.

Evidence on everything

Records used, method run, confidence, step-by-step reasoning path.

Networks & gangs

Community detection finds gangs; link prediction suggests hidden ties.

Forecasting

Near-repeat space-time scan predicts where a series strikes next.

Proactive briefings

Nightly auto-intelligence pushed to each station (Catalyst Cron).
