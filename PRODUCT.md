# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Karnataka State Police investigators, crime analysts, supervisors, and SCRB policymakers.
Context: interrogating the state crime database under time pressure during active
investigations, briefings, and strategic reviews — often by officers with limited technical
skill or English fluency. The job to be done: ask a question in plain language (English or
Kannada, typed or spoken) and get an explainable, evidence-backed, visual answer in seconds.

## Product Purpose
NETRA turns a siloed crime database into a conversational intelligence copilot. Every answer is
a living visual (hotspot map, criminal-network graph, offender risk dossier, money trail) with a
clickable evidence trail back to the exact FIRs used. Success = an officer trusts, understands,
and acts on the answer without needing an analyst or a query language. Built for the KSP
Datathon 2026 (Problem Statement 1), deployed on Zoho Catalyst.

## Brand Personality
Authoritative, precise, trustworthy. Three words: **vigilant, lucid, accountable.** It should
feel like a calm command-centre instrument — serious government-grade tooling, not a consumer
chatbot. Confidence without flash; every claim shows its evidence.

## Anti-references
- Generic purple-gradient SaaS chatbots / "AI assistant" landing aesthetics.
- Toy demo dashboards with rainbow charts and decorative glassmorphism everywhere.
- Cluttered enterprise crime software (dense grey tables, no hierarchy).
- Anything that looks playful, gamified, or startup-cute — this is law enforcement.

## Design Principles
1. **Evidence over assertion** — the reasoning trail and source records are first-class UI, never hidden.
2. **The answer is a picture** — visual surface leads; text supports.
3. **Calm command centre** — dark, focused, low-chrome; information density without noise.
4. **Governed by design** — role, redaction, and audit are visible, not buried.
5. **Bilingual dignity** — Kannada is a first-class language, not an afterthought toggle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1 on the dark theme. Kannada (Noto Sans Kannada)
and English render cleanly. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all transitions/pulse animations.
Keyboard-navigable chat and controls. Colour is never the sole carrier of meaning (risk bands
pair colour with a number/label; predicted edges pair colour with a dashed style + label).
